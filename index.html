news about our school

  • Our School

    Owliver's Post brought to the middle schoolers of The Deens Academy, Whitefield campus, an innovative and interactive approach of familiarizing them with the current happenings in the world. The students of grade 7 truly enjoyed this experience as they got to don the hats of detectives and solve a multitude of clues and puzzles all the while working as a team.

  • Sports

    Playing games is healthy, fun and physically invigorating. At our school, outdoor activities and physical education go hand-in-hand with the rest of the curriculum. Sport is practiced in its many forms and in all its diversity at Deens. Students learn a variety of games teamed with training in athletics, football, cricket, basketball and disciplining via Mass PT.
